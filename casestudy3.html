Project Overview

The Product

Animal Shelter is an App to the users to adopt a pet online with the process being completed in person.

The Duration

Start : August 2022

End : November 2022

The Problem

It is difficult to get a real feel for the animal's personality online, as most animals are not given a description and only has one picture, taken when they first get there, in which they are often terrified.

The Goal


Designing a website whree users to help potential pet parents make decisions based more on personality since being able to see an animal is important to both people.

My Role


My role is UX researcher and UX designer

My Responsibility

Creating digital and paper wireframe, creating low and high fidelity prototype, conducting usability studies, conducting interviews and interacting on designs.

Personas

Problem statement:
Lauren is a mother of a 2-year-old and a 7-year-old, the older of which has recently expressed interest in having a pet. Growing up with both a cat and dog, Lauren is in favor of this idea, but knows not all animals get along with young children. Because of this, she is reluctant to go meet animals in person with her children until she views their profiles online and determines it is safe for her children to interact with the animal.
